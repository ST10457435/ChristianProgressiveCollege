Primary School Education

At Christian Progressive College, we proudly offer primary education from Grade RR through to Grade 7. Our curriculum is designed to nurture the intellectual, emotional, and social growth of every learner in a safe and supportive environment.

We believe that the early years form the foundation for lifelong learning. Our highly qualified teachers use innovative teaching methods, engaging activities, and state-of-the-art resources to ensure your child receives the best start in their educational journey.
