High School Education

Our high school program, catering from Grade 8 through Grade 12, is designed to challenge and inspire students as they prepare for tertiary education and future careers. With a rigorous academic curriculum and a wide range of extracurricular opportunities, we ensure that every student is well-prepared for the challenges ahead.

At Christian Progressive College, we combine academic excellence with personal growth. Our experienced teachers and modern facilities create a nurturing environment where every learner can achieve their full potential.
