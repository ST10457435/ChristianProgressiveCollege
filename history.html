Our History

Christian Progressive College has been a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development for over 20 years. Our rich history is marked by continuous growth, innovation in teaching, and a steadfast commitment to nurturing the leaders of tomorrow.

Over the decades, we have evolved with the changing educational landscape, consistently updating our curriculum and facilities to meet modern standards. Our alumni have gone on to excel in various fields, a testament to our enduring legacy of quality education.
