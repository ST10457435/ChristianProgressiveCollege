Discover Excellence at Christian Progressive College

At Christian Progressive College, we believe that every student has the potential to achieve greatness. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our outstanding academic results—with an overall matric pass rate exceeding 90% for the past three years. We take pride in nurturing a learning environment that not only emphasizes scholastic achievement but also encourages personal growth and character development.

While we offer a range of extra-curricular activities that enrich our students’ school life, our true focus remains on academic excellence. We provide innovative teaching methods, a robust curriculum, and dedicated staff to ensure that every child is given the best possible education. Our comprehensive programs are designed to equip students with the skills they need to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Parents looking for a school that delivers academic brilliance alongside a supportive and engaging community will find Christian Progressive College to be one of the best choices available. In addition to our standard curriculum, we also offer matric rewrites, ensuring that every student has the opportunity to improve and excel.

Join us at Christian Progressive College and become part of a community where education meets passion, and academic achievement is celebrated every day. We are more than just a school—we are a launchpad for future leaders and innovators.
