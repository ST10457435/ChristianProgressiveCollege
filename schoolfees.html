Aftercare Services

At Christian Progressive College, we understand that learning doesn’t stop when the school day ends. That’s why we offer comprehensive aftercare services designed to support our learners both academically and personally.

Our aftercare program includes dedicated sessions where experienced teachers help students with homework, provide extra tutoring, and offer guidance on projects and assignments. We also organize engaging activities that foster teamwork, creativity, and social skills in a relaxed environment.

With our aftercare services, parents can have peace of mind knowing that their children are receiving the additional support they need to excel. Our goal is to nurture well-rounded individuals who are prepared for both academic challenges and real-life opportunities.
